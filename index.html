Services

Bookingkeeping

We offer a complete bookkeeping service, both in house and on site. Our staff are fully trained on manual as well as computerised packages and transform those carrier bags and shoe boxes in to an accurate trial balance from which statutory accounts can be produced.

Taxation

Tax computation is done after annual accounts have been finalised and our experienced staff ensures that full use of statutory concessions and allowances is made in order to minimise tax liability. Advance tax planning is also available when required.

Cashflow

Most lending institutions and banks will look for a business plan and cash flow statement when considering any type of lending. We at Mian & Co will spend some time with you and work with you in order to prepare and develop that plan for you to be able to obtain the funding that you require either for start up or for growth purposes.

Limited Companies & Incorporations

If you are at a point where you wish to incorporate your business and need assistance in setting up a limited company then come and talk to us. We at Mian & Co will explain in detail advantages and disadvantages of a Limited Company, role of a Director, Secretary and Shareholder. We shall be happy to guide you in the right direction to set up your own Limited Company.

Self Assessments

Most one man bands / small business’s find it a nightmare to deal with Self Assessment forms issued by HM Revenue & Customs. If you feel that you are having difficulty filling those forms and need some help then call us. We can take that burden away from you leaving you free to go out and generate business/income.

Value Added Tax

VAT is one of the most complex indirect taxes and we offer expert help to ensure that you do not fall foul of the regulations. We at Mian & Co take time to understand your business operations and apply an appropriate VAT scheme to minimise the amount of VAT you pay.

About

Fortress Accountacy is located in the heart of Leicester City and free car parking is available to visitors.

We have been established in Leicester since 2014, our clients continually praise the quality of our service and much of our business is achieved by word of mouth.

Working as a team with our clients, we ensure there are no surprises from the Inland Revenue at the end of the financial year. We offer a very personalised service and are willing to go that extra mile to help our clients achieve their financial goals.

We excel at looking after small to medium sized business in all sectors, including manufacturing, children’s nursery owners, doctors, dentists, pharmacists and charity organisations.
